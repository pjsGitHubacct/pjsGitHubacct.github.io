Agentforce – Sales Cloud

Autonomous AI Agents for Salesforce Sales Cloud

📌 Overview

This repository contains sample configurations, documentation, and implementation guidance for building Agentforce AI agents in Salesforce Sales Cloud.

🚀 Use Cases

  • Inbound Lead Qualification
  • Lead Nurturing Automation
  • Opportunity Stage & Next‑Step Suggestions
  • Pipeline Management & Forecast Accuracy
  • Sales Coaching with AI Agents

🧱 Architecture

  • Salesforce Agentforce
  • Sales Cloud (Lead, Opportunity, Activity)
  • Prompt Templates
  • Salesforce Flow & Actions
  • Data Cloud (optional, recommended)
